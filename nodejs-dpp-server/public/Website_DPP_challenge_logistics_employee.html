Maintenance Information

Proper maintenance of the EUV Wafer Stage Precision Motor ensures optimal performance and longevity. Follow the guidelines below:

Regular Cleaning

Clean the motor components with a lint-free cloth and isopropyl alcohol every 6 months to prevent dust accumulation.

Lubrication

Apply high-grade lubricant to moving parts annually to reduce wear and tear.

Calibration

Ensure the motor is recalibrated by certified technicians every 12 months to maintain precision.

Inspection

Conduct a thorough inspection of all components every 2 years to identify and replace worn-out parts.
